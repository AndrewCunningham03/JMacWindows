How the round works

1. Tap "Done" on a customer once you've cleaned their windows. It records the date and (if you collected cash) marks them paid.

2. They stay "done" until the next round starts — even if you cleaned them yesterday, not just today. The ✓ and date next to their name show when it was actually done.

3. "↻ Next round" (in the Routes tab, admin only) resets a whole route back to not-done and unpaid, ready to clean again. Nothing is deleted — every past job is still saved in History.

4. "Overdue" just means it's been longer than usual since their last clean (35 days for monthly, 70 for every-2-months).
